Mindset Assessment

1. I believe that intelligence is largely inborn and cannot be changed very much.

2. I believe that no matter what kind of person you are, it is always possible to make major changes for the better.

3. I believe it’s possible to substantially increase how intelligent you are.

4. I don’t think people can change who they truly are at their core.

5. It is always possible to change the basic things about the kind of person you are.

6. I think musical ability is something that anyone can learn.

7. Talent is something you are born with, not something you can develop.

8. I believe math is much easier to learn if you are male or come from a culture that values math.

9. If I stay focused, work harder and smarter, I can become the best at a particular skill regardless of natural ability.

10. No matter what kind of person you are, you can always change substantially.

11. Trying new things is stressful for me, so I try to avoid it.

12. Some people are good and kind, some aren’t. They rarely change.

13. I value it when others give me feedback, even if negative.

14. I dislike criticism because I have high standards.

15. All human beings are capable of the same amount of learning.

16. You can learn new things, but it won’t change intelligence.

17. You can choose to act differently, but talents can't be changed.

18. People are fundamentally good, but sometimes make bad choices.

19. I study because I like learning and gaining skills.

20. Some people are born smart and don’t need to work hard.
